Pricing FAQ

Common questions about our pricing structure and billing policies

We charge based on the actual audio length, rounded up to the nearest minute. For example, a 5 minutes and 30 seconds recording is billed as 6 minutes. We provide detailed time logs with each transcript showing exact audio duration.

No setup fees, no hidden costs. Our transparent pricing includes all standard features: transcription, formatting, speaker identification, confidentiality protocols, and one round of revisions. Additional services like timestamping or multilingual support have clearly stated add-on rates.

Volume discounts are calculated on a rolling 12-month basis. If your average monthly usage over the past year qualifies for a discount tier, you receive that discount rate immediately. No long-term contracts required - discounts adjust automatically as your usage changes.

For projects over 4 hours of audio requiring same-day delivery, we provide custom enterprise rush pricing that's typically 30-40% below our standard rush rates. Contact our team for immediate consultation and capacity confirmation. We can deploy multiple transcriptionists to meet tight deadlines.
