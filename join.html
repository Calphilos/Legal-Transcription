Join Verdilex Transcription Services – Legal Transcriber

Verdilex Transcription Services is seeking qualified legal transcribers to join our team. As a legal transcriber, you will play a critical role in producing accurate, high-quality transcripts for a variety of legal proceedings. We are looking for detail-oriented professionals who are committed to excellence and can work independently to meet deadlines.

Qualifications

Candidates must:

  • Hold a Bachelor’s degree in any field.
  • Have a dedicated Windows computer equipped with Microsoft Word 2010 or higher and be confident in its use.
  • Have a stable Internet connection.
  • Have excellent command of the English language, including grammar, punctuation, and style.
  • Be confident in using technology to produce documents to the highest standard.
  • Be comfortable researching case information and references online.
  • Be committed to producing high-quality, accurate transcripts and ensuring deadlines are met.
  • Possess strong communication skills to ensure the best possible service.
  • Be available to work on a freelance (self-employed) basis, understanding that workload may vary.

Relevant Skills

  • Native-level proficiency in English and proficiency in another language is a plus.
  • Experience in one or more fields such as legal, medical, technical, audiovisual, or literary transcription.
  • Familiarity with CAT tools (Computer-Assisted Translation tools) is an advantage.
  • Strong attention to detail, research skills, and the ability to meet deadlines consistently.
  • At least 1 year of professional transcription or translation experience.

If this opportunity aligns with your skills and experience, we invite you to apply below!
